History of Mercedes-Benz

In 1901, he created the Benz Patent Motorwagen, which was the first gasoline car and introduced it to the public under Daimler Motors Corporation. Then, in 1902, an Austrian automobile entrepreneur, Emil Jellinek, trademarked the name “Mercedes” after Mercedes Jellinek, his daughter.

What is the basic information about Mercedes-Benz?

It develops, manufactures and distributes premium and luxury cars and vans. The company also offers various services such as financing, leasing, car subscription, car rental, fleet management, insurance brokerage, digital services for charging and payment, besides innovative mobility services.
